Grayscale to Coloured Image

Image Colorization using cGAN is a project aimed at transforming grayscale images into colourized images. Using a cGAN, I developed and trained a model specifically designed for image colorization.

To improve the model's performance, I implemented skip connections within the U-Net architecture, enabling better feature extraction and resulting in more accurate and detailed colorization results. Through extensive hyperparameter tuning, I optimized the model's performance, showcasing my understanding of how different parameters impact training and outcomes.

Rather than relying on the traditional RGB color space, I leveraged the LAB color space representation, offering greater flexibility in capturing perceptual differences between colors.
